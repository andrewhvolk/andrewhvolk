Welcome

The Liberty University Math Club & Honor Society brings together students passionate about mathematics, problem-solving, and academic excellence. We hold meetings, workshops, and events designed to challenge your thinking and build lasting professional networks.

  • Collaborative Problem-Solving Sessions
  • Guest Speakers & Faculty Seminars
  • Mathematics Competitions & Conferences
  • Academic Mentoring & Community Outreach

Membership

Membership is open to all Liberty University students interested in pursuing advanced study in mathematics. Admission to the Math Honor Society may require a certain GPA, coursework completion, or recommendation from faculty. Further details will be announced at our first meeting of the semester.
