MATH 121: College Algebra

Comprehensive study of algebraic operations and functional relationships.

Course Overview

This course offers an in-depth exploration of algebraic operations, including linear and nonlinear equations, functions, and problem-solving techniques essential for higher-level mathematics.

Syllabus

  • Week 1: Review of Algebraic Concepts
  • Week 2: Linear Equations and Inequalities
  • Week 3: Polynomial Functions and Factoring
  • Week 4: Rational Functions and Exponential Models
  • Week 5: Advanced Problem Solving
  • Week 6: Comprehensive Review
