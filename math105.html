MATH 105: Algebra for Non-STEM Majors

Practical algebra skills for everyday problem-solving.

Course Overview

This course is designed to introduce basic algebra concepts with practical applications, ensuring that students develop problem-solving skills for real-world scenarios.

Syllabus

  • Week 1: Introduction to Algebraic Expressions
  • Week 2: Solving Equations and Inequalities
  • Week 3: Real-Life Applications of Algebra
  • Week 4: Graphing Fundamentals
  • Week 5: Problem-Solving Strategies
  • Week 6: Course Review and Assessment
