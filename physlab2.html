PHYS 202L/232L: Physics Lab

Interactive investigations in electricity, magnetism, and optics.

Course Overview

This course emphasizes hands-on experiments in electricity, magnetism, and optics, aiming to build a robust foundational understanding through interactive learning.

Syllabus

  • Week 1: Electrical Circuits and Measurements
  • Week 2: Magnetism and Electromagnetic Fields
  • Week 3: Optics and Light Behavior
  • Week 4: Advanced Circuit Analysis
  • Week 5: Integrated Lab Experiments
  • Week 6: Final Project and Assessment
